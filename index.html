Goal: Sink all your opponent's ships before they sink yours.
                    
Placing Ships: 
Left Click: Place ships horizontally. 
Right Click: Place ships vertically.
Ships cannot overlap or go outside the grid.

Gameplay:
Right Click: Mark cells to keep track of your guesses.
Players take turns to attack by clicking on a grid coordinate. 
Hits and misses are marked on the grid.
A ship is sunk when all its squares are hit.

Winning:
The first player to sink all opponent's ships wins.
